# Skill: Follow-Up Messages

## Trigger
Use this skill when Kerry needs a follow-up message — for car sales leads, business contacts, or note deal outreach. Delivers ready-to-send templates with cadence guidance.

## Inputs Needed
- Scenario: which template type (see below)
- Any known details: name, vehicle interest, last interaction date, context

---

## Car Sales Follow-Up Templates

### 1. Fresh Lead — Same Day (No Contact Yet)
> "Hey [Name], this is Kerry at Clay Cooley Volkswagen in Richardson. I saw you were checking out [vehicle/our inventory] — I'd love to help you find the right fit. No pressure, just want to make sure you get what you're actually looking for. What's a good time to connect?"

*If no response after 4 hours:*
> "Hey [Name] — Kerry again from Clay Cooley VW. Totally understand if you're busy. I'll be here all week — just shoot me a text whenever works for you. Happy to answer questions over text too."

---

### 2. Appointment No-Show
Send within 1 hour of missed appointment:
> "Hey [Name], Kerry here from Clay Cooley VW — looks like we missed each other today. No worries at all. When's a good time to reschedule? I'll make sure I've got the right options pulled for you."

*If no response after 24 hours:*
> "Hey [Name], just following up on rescheduling. Still have some solid options I think you'd like. What day works better for you this week?"

---

### 3. Visited But Didn't Buy
Send within 2 hours of departure:
> "Hey [Name], Kerry here — appreciate you coming in today. I know it's a big decision and I want to make sure you feel right about it. If any questions come up or you want to revisit the numbers, I'm just a text away."

*3-day follow-up:*
> "Hey [Name], Kerry from Clay Cooley. Just checking in — still thinking about the [vehicle]? I had another customer looking at the same one, wanted to give you first shot before it moves. Let me know."

---

### 4. Post-Purchase Check-In + Referral Ask
Send 48 hours after delivery:
> "Hey [Name], hope you're loving the [vehicle]! It was great working with you. If you know anyone — friend, family, coworker — who's in the market, I'd love the introduction. I take good care of people my customers send my way. Enjoy the ride."

---

### 5. Cold Lead Reactivation

**30-Day:**
> "Hey [Name], Kerry from Clay Cooley VW — been a minute. Just wanted to check in. Still in the market? Inventory has changed a lot and I might have something you'd actually like now."

**60-Day:**
> "Hey [Name], Kerry here. I know life gets busy. If you're still thinking about getting into something, I can make it a quick and painless process. Worth a 10-minute conversation?"

**90-Day:**
> "Hey [Name] — last follow-up from me. Kerry at Clay Cooley VW. If the timing is ever right, I'm here. No pressure at all — just wanted you to know you've got someone in your corner when you're ready."

---

## Note Business Follow-Up Templates

### Seller Outreach Follow-Up
After initial contact with a note seller or servicer:
> "Hey [Name], Kerry Kelley here — following up on our conversation about [asset/tape/deal]. I've had a chance to review the details and I'm still interested in moving forward. What's the best next step on your end?"

*If no response after 5 business days:*
> "Hey [Name], Kerry again. I know these deals move on their timeline — just wanted to stay on your radar. If the situation on [asset] changes or you have other inventory, I'd love to be one of your first calls."

---

## Cadence Guide

| Scenario | Touch 1 | Touch 2 | Touch 3 | Stop |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fresh internet lead | Immediate | +4 hours | +24 hours | Day 3 |
| No-show | Within 1 hour | +24 hours | +3 days | Day 7 |
| Visited, no sale | Within 2 hours | +3 days | +7 days | Day 14 |
| Cold lead | 30 days | 60 days | 90 days | Done |
| Note seller | Same day | +5 business days | +10 days | Day 21 |

---

## Output Format
Deliver messages ready to copy-paste with no editing required. Include the customer's name placeholder in brackets. Flag which communication channel each message is best suited for (text, email, call).
