# Skill: Social Media Management

## Trigger
Use this skill when Kerry needs to create, batch, or plan social media content across platforms — for Standard Black brand or his car sales role.

## Inputs Needed
- Track: Standard Black brand OR car sales (Clay Cooley VW)
- Platforms: specify which (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, or all)
- Volume: single post OR full content batch (1–2 weeks)
- Any specific topic, angle, or event to build around (optional)

---

## Two Content Tracks

### Track 1: Standard Black Brand
Goal: Build authority, credibility, and audience around Kerry's vision.

**Content pillars:**
1. **Authority** — Insights on investing, business building, wealth strategy (no fluff)
2. **Behind the scenes** — Real documentation of the build; what it actually takes
3. **Culture** — Team, values, the Standard Black identity; who we are and why it matters
4. **Value** — Tactical takeaways: real estate notes, business ownership, generational wealth thinking

**Tone:** Confident, plain-spoken, visionary. Sounds like a man building something serious, not a content creator.

**Posting cadence (recommended):**
- Instagram: 4–5x/week (mix of Reels and static posts)
- TikTok: 3–5x/week (short-form video, native feel)
- LinkedIn: 2–3x/week (longer-form insights, professional tone)
- Facebook: 3x/week (mirror Instagram or repurpose)

---

### Track 2: Car Sales (Clay Cooley VW)
Goal: Generate inbound leads and appointment requests from local buyers.

**Content pillars:**
1. **Inventory highlights** — Specific vehicles with price, features, and a direct CTA
2. **Social proof** — Customer deliveries (with permission), happy buyer moments
3. **Education** — "What to know before buying a car," financing myths, trade-in tips
4. **Personal brand** — Kerry as a real, trustworthy salesperson people want to work with

**Tone:** Genuine, warm, direct. Southern hospitality — not a pushy pitch.

**Posting cadence (recommended):**
- Facebook / Marketplace: Daily inventory posts
- Instagram: 3x/week
- TikTok: 2–3x/week (walkaround videos, delivery moments, tips)

## Content Batching Workflow

When Kerry asks for a content batch, deliver:

1. **Confirm scope** — Track (brand or car sales), platforms, timeframe (1 week = 5–7 posts, 2 weeks = 10–14)
2. **Draft post library** — Each post includes:
   - Platform
   - Caption (ready to copy-paste)
   - Visual direction (what to film or photograph)
   - Hashtags (platform-appropriate)
   - Suggested post day/time
3. **Flag video posts** — Note which ones require Kerry to film something vs. static image

---

## Platform-Specific Rules

### Instagram
- Caption: Hook in line 1 (before "more" cutoff). Max 2,200 characters but keep it under 300 for feed posts.
- Hashtags: 5–10, relevant, mix of niche and broad
- Reels: Short (15–30 sec), high energy opening, text overlay, trending audio when relevant
- Stories: Use for real-time, informal, behind-the-scenes

### TikTok
- First 2–3 seconds must hook or they scroll
- Speak directly to camera — authenticity beats production value
- Text overlay on key lines
- No hard sell — entertain, educate, or document
- Post at 7–9am or 6–9pm CST for best reach

### LinkedIn
- Longer posts perform well — 150–300 words
- Lead with a bold first line (no greeting, no "I'm excited to share")
- Professional but personal — insight + real story
- No hashtag overload — 3–5 max
- Best days: Tuesday–Thursday

### Facebook
- Short posts with a clear CTA perform best for car sales
- Marketplace listings are separate from page posts — use both
- Share Reels/TikToks natively for extended reach

---

## Output Format
For single posts: deliver caption + visual direction + hashtags, platform-labeled.
For batches: deliver a numbered content calendar with all fields above, organized by day and platform.
