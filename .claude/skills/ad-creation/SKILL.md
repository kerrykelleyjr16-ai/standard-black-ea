# Skill: Ad Creation

## Trigger
Use this skill when Kerry needs to create an ad — for car sales at Clay Cooley VW or for Standard Black brand awareness. Covers copy, creative direction, and platform formatting.

## Inputs Needed
- Use case: car sales OR Standard Black
- Platform: Meta (FB/IG), TikTok Ads, Google Ads, or Craigslist/Marketplace
- Product/offer: specific vehicle, general inventory, brand message, or service
- Audience: who this is targeting
- Goal: leads, awareness, appointments, clicks
- Budget (optional — helps with format recommendation)

---

## Platform Specs & Format Rules

### Meta (Facebook / Instagram)
- **Headline:** 40 characters max (punchy, benefit-forward)
- **Primary text:** 125 characters before "See More" cutoff — hook must land in first line
- **Description:** 30 characters max
- **CTA options:** Learn More, Contact Us, Get Quote, Book Now, Message Page
- **Creative:** High-contrast image or 15–30 sec video; no more than 20% text on image
- **Best for:** Lead gen, retargeting, appointment booking

### TikTok Ads
- **Copy:** Very short — hook in first 3 seconds, 1–2 lines of text overlay
- **Format:** Video only (9:16 vertical, 15–60 seconds ideal)
- **Tone:** Native, casual — should NOT look like an ad
- **CTA:** Simple action (visit, DM, call)
- **Best for:** Brand awareness, younger buyers, social proof content

### Google Ads (Search)
- **Headline:** Up to 3 headlines, 30 characters each
- **Description:** Up to 2 descriptions, 90 characters each
- **Keywords:** Include in headlines naturally
- **Best for:** High-intent buyers actively searching

### Craigslist / Facebook Marketplace
- **Title:** Clear, specific — Year Make Model Trim Price (for cars)
- **Body:** Condition, key features, price, contact info, call to action
- **Photos:** 10+ photos, clean backgrounds, all angles
- **Tone:** Straightforward, no hype — buyers are deal-hunting
- **Best for:** Local, price-sensitive, direct response

---

## Workflow

1. **Get the brief** — Use case, platform, offer, audience, goal
2. **Write primary copy** — Headline + body + CTA
3. **Write creative direction** — Describe the ideal image or video concept (what's shown, what's said, energy/tone)
4. **Format for platform** — Apply character limits and specs
5. **A/B variant** (optional) — Alternate headline or first line to split test

## Car Sales Ad Frameworks

**Lead gen / appointment:**
> Headline: "Drive Home This Weekend"
> Body: "Looking for your next car without the hassle? Come see us at Clay Cooley Volkswagen in Richardson. New and pre-owned inventory, real deals, no pressure. Book your visit today."
> CTA: Book Now / Message Us

**Inventory specific:**
> Headline: "[Year] [Model] — [Price or Payment]"
> Body: "[Key features]. [Mileage if used]. Clean title. Ready to go. Come see it in person or call/text Kerry directly at [###]."
> CTA: Contact Us

**Social proof:**
> "Another one delivered. [Customer first name] drove home in their new [vehicle] today. You could be next — DM me or stop by Clay Cooley VW in Richardson."
