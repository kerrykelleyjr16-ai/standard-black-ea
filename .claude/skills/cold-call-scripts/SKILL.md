# Skill: Cold Call Scripts

## Trigger
Use this skill when Kerry needs a phone script — for car sales outreach, internet lead callbacks, or note business prospecting. Delivers full scripts with objection handling and voicemail versions.

## Inputs Needed
- Use case: car sales (internet lead, referral, cold prospect) OR note business (seller, servicer, broker)
- Any known context: name, vehicle interest, source, prior contact

---

## Car Sales Scripts

### Script 1: Internet Lead Callback (First Attempt)

> **Kerry:** "Hey, is this [Name]?"
>
> **[Yes]** → "Hey [Name], this is Kerry calling from Clay Cooley Volkswagen in Richardson — you submitted a request online about [vehicle/inventory]. I just wanted to reach out personally, make sure you get the right information and not just a form response. You got a couple minutes?"
>
> **[Yes]** → "Awesome. So what caught your eye — was it a specific car or were you more just browsing to see what's out there?"
>
> *[Listen. Qualify from their answer.]*
>
> "Got it. So here's what I'd suggest — come in, let me put you in a couple options that actually match what you're describing. No pressure, no games. If it works, great. If not, at least you've seen it in person. What does your schedule look like this week?"

**Voicemail version:**
> "Hey [Name], Kerry calling from Clay Cooley Volkswagen — you reached out about [vehicle]. I want to make sure you're taken care of personally, not just through the system. Give me a call back at [###-###-####] or shoot me a text — whichever is easier. I'm here all week."

---

### Script 2: Referral Call

> "Hey [Name], my name is Kerry — I work at Clay Cooley Volkswagen in Richardson. [Referral name] mentioned you might be in the market for a car. I'm not calling to pitch you — I just like to reach out personally when someone sends me a referral because I take that seriously. Are you actually looking, or was [referral] jumping the gun?"
>
> *[If yes, looking]* → "Perfect. Tell me what you're looking for — I'll be straight with you about whether I can help."

---

### Script 3: Unsold Customer Follow-Up Call

> "Hey [Name], Kerry from Clay Cooley — we met [last week / a few days ago] when you came in to look at [vehicle]. Just wanted to follow up personally, not with some automated email. How are you feeling about everything? Still considering it?"
>
> *[If hesitant]* → "What's the main thing that's holding you back? I want to make sure it's not something I can actually help with before you move on."

---

### Top 5 Car Sales Objections

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I'm just browsing" | "That's fine — most of my best deals start that way. What would have to be true for it to make sense today?" |
| "I need to think about it" | "Totally. What's the one thing you'd need to feel good about before saying yes?" |
| "The payment is too high" | "Let's work it. What's your target? I'll see what we can do — I'd rather find something that fits than lose you." |
| "I need to talk to my spouse" | "Makes sense. Can we get them on the phone now? I'd hate for you to make a second trip." |
| "I'm checking other dealerships" | "Smart move. Here's my number — call me last. I'll match or beat what you find, and I'll make it easier." |

## Note Business Scripts

### Script 1: Seller/Servicer Outreach

> "Hey [Name], my name is Kerry Kelley — I'm a note investor based out of Texas. I came across [your name / your company] and wanted to reach out directly. I focus on residential first-lien notes — performing and reperforming. Do you work with buyers directly, or is there a better process for me to go through?"
>
> *[If open]* → "Great. I'm disciplined on my buy box and I close what I put under contract. What does your typical tape look like in terms of volume and geography?"

**Voicemail:**
> "Hey [Name], Kerry Kelley calling — I'm a residential note investor based in Texas. I focus on first-lien performing and reperforming paper. I'd love to get on your radar for future tape opportunities. My number is [###-###-####]. Happy to connect whenever works."

### Script 2: Broker Outreach

> "Hey [Name], Kerry Kelley here. I invest in residential mortgage notes — first-lien, SFR and small multifamily. I'm building out my source network and wanted to connect. Do you broker tape, or are you more on the origination side?"

---

## Output Format
Deliver scripts in dialogue format — ready to read directly. Include voicemail version for every outbound script. Flag where to listen and adapt vs. where to stick to the script.
