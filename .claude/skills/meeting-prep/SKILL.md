# Skill: Meeting Prep

## Trigger
Use this skill when Kerry has a meeting coming up and needs it structured — agenda, context package, or post-meeting capture. Also use for team check-ins and investor/partner conversations.

## Inputs Needed
- Who is in the meeting
- What the meeting is about / the objective
- When (date and time)
- Any prior context, open items, or decisions that are relevant
- Format: internal team check-in, external business meeting, investor conversation, or one-on-one

---

## Meeting Types

### 1. Internal Team Check-In (Kerry, Kody, TJ)
Goal: Keep the team aligned, surface blockers, move things forward.

**Suggested cadence:** Weekly, 30–45 minutes

**Standard agenda:**
```
TEAM CHECK-IN — [Date]

1. Wins from last week (5 min)
   - Kerry:
   - Kody:
   - TJ:

2. Priority review (10 min)
   - What are the top 3 things we're working on this week?
   - Is anything off track?

3. Open items / blockers (10 min)
   - What does each person need to move forward?

4. Decisions needed (10 min)
   - Any decisions the team needs to make today?

5. Next steps (5 min)
   - Who owns what before next check-in?
```

## Post-Meeting Capture
Run this after every significant meeting:

```
POST-MEETING NOTES — [Date]
Meeting: [Topic] with [Name(s)]

## What Was Decided
-
-

## Action Items
- [ ] [Task] — Owner: [Name] — Due: [Date]
- [ ] [Task] — Owner: [Name] — Due: [Date]

## Open Items (Unresolved)
-

## Log to decisions/log.md?
[Yes / No — if yes, summarize the decision to append]
```

---

## Workflow

1. Get meeting details (who, what, when, objective)
2. Pull relevant context from projects/, decisions/log.md, or prior conversations
3. Select the right meeting format above
4. Draft the agenda or prep package
5. After the meeting: run post-meeting capture and flag anything for the decision log

---

## Output Format
Deliver the full prep document ready to use — not a template with blanks, but a filled-in, specific document based on what Kerry told me. If I'm missing key context, ask one question before drafting.
