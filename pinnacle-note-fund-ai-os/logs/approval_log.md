# Approval Log — The Pinnacle Note Fund

**Maintained By:** Agent 01 (Chief Operating Coordinator)
**Format:** Append-only. Every human approval required by the Human Approval Matrix is logged here.
**Purpose:** Audit trail for all authorized decisions. Demonstrates compliance with the Human Approval Matrix.

---

## Log Format

```
[YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM] | TYPE: [Approval Type] | REQUESTED BY: [Agent / Person] | APPROVED BY: [Human approver(s)] | ITEM: [Description of what was approved] | CONDITIONS: [Any conditions or modifications] | FINAL STATUS: [Approved / Rejected / Approved with Modification]
```
