## Severity Classification

| Severity | Definition | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Threatens enforceability of lien, title, or fund's legal rights; threatens fund's ability to close or foreclose | 5 business days |
| **Major** | Financial impact likely; closing risk; regulatory risk; significant operational failure | 15 business days |
| **Minor** | Administrative gap; low financial impact; no closing risk | 30 business days |
| **Informational** | Logged for awareness; no financial or legal impact; no mandatory deadline | Track only |
