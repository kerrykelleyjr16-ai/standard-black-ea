# Marketing Review Policy

**Fund:** The Pinnacle Note Fund
**Effective Date:** [DATE]
**Approved By:** CEO/CIO
**Compliance Review:** Agent 14 (Compliance, Marketing Review & Disclosure)

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the review, approval, and release of all investor-facing, prospective investor-facing, and public-facing materials produced by or on behalf of The Pinnacle Note Fund. No material bypasses this process.

---

## 2. Materials Subject to Review

The following materials require compliance review and CEO/CIO approval before release:

- Pitch decks and investor presentations
- One-page summaries and teaser materials
- Private Placement Memorandum (PPM) and any updates
- Website content (fund or manager website)
- Email campaigns and outreach sequences
- Social media posts that reference the fund, its strategy, or its performance
- Investor reports (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Distribution notices
- DDQ and RFP responses
- Any press release or public statement related to the fund
- Any co-branded or co-marketing materials with placement agents or capital-raising partners

---

## 3. Review Workflow

All materials follow the Compliance Review Workflow (WF-07):

1. Material drafted (Agent 16, Agent 17, or CEO/CIO)
2. Submitted to Agent 14 with: document type, intended audience, intended channel, urgency
3. Agent 14 completes compliance review
4. Agent 14 delivers findings and required edits
5. Originating agent incorporates required edits
6. Agent 14 confirms clearance
7. CEO/CIO approves release
8. Material released through approved channel
9. Compliance log updated

**No material bypasses this sequence.**

---

## 4. Performance Claims

All performance data in any material must comply with the following:

- **Gross vs. Net:** Both gross and net returns must be disclosed wherever performance is shown. "Net" means after all fund-level fees and expenses.
- **Time Period:** The time period must be clearly labeled. Cherry-picked periods are prohibited.
- **Verification:** All performance data must be verified against the fund's official accounting records (Agent 10 confirmation required before compliance clearance)
- **Audited vs. Unaudited:** Unaudited data must be labeled as such
- **Past performance disclaimer:** Required on all materials containing any performance figures:
  *"Past performance is not necessarily indicative of future results."*
- **Track record from prior funds/roles:** Must be clearly labeled as "prior fund" or "as prior manager" and must only be shown when consistent with SEC staff guidance on track record attribution

---

## 5. Prohibited Language

The following language is prohibited in any fund material without exception:

| Prohibited | Acceptable Alternative |
|---|---|
| "Guaranteed returns" | "Targeted returns" or "historical return" |
| "Risk-free" | "Lower risk" (only with full qualification) |
| "Safe investment" | [Avoid; describe risk profile accurately] |
| "You will receive X%" | "The fund targets X% returns" |
| "We always generate…" | "The fund has historically generated…" |
| "The best mortgage note fund" | "A leading" or [omit superlatives] |
| "No risk of loss" | [Never — always describe loss risk] |
| "Consistent returns" (without qualification) | "The fund has generated consistent returns in its operating history [period]" |
| "SEC-registered" (if not) | "The fund is exempt from registration under Regulation D" |
| "Accredited investors only" (as a selling point) | Use as a factual eligibility statement, not a performance claim |

---

## 6. Disclosure Requirements

All investor materials must include or reference the following:

- Risk factor summary (or reference to full risk factors in PPM)
- Fee disclosure (management fee, carried interest, expenses)
- Conflict of interest disclosure summary
- Accredited investor eligibility requirement (for Reg D offerings)
- Past performance disclaimer (if any performance data is shown)
- Forward-looking statement disclaimer (if any projections are included)
- Fund counsel and compliance counsel contact (if required by state law)
- No-guarantee-of-returns statement

---

## 7. Social Media Policy

Social media represents significant regulatory risk for private funds. Rules:

- No fund social media post may go live without prior compliance review and CEO/CIO approval
- No performance data may be posted on social media (even with disclaimers — the format cannot support adequate disclosure)
- Fund social media may describe: fund strategy (general), team background (factual), real estate/note market commentary, educational content about mortgage notes — with compliance review
- General solicitation rules apply: if the fund is conducting a Reg D 506(b) offering (no general solicitation), social media posts that could be interpreted as a solicitation are prohibited
- Agent 14 reviews all planned social content before posting

---

## 8. Website Policy

- The fund's website (if any) must display all required disclaimers on every page
- No performance data may appear on publicly accessible website pages without compliance review and current data verification
- "Contact us" forms that collect investor information must include appropriate risk disclosures
- Agent 14 reviews the fund website annually and any time material changes are made

---

## 9. Placement Agent and Third-Party Marketing

If a placement agent or third-party capital-raising partner creates materials referencing the fund:

- All such materials must be submitted to Agent 14 for compliance review before use
- The fund is responsible for reviewing materials used by authorized placement agents
- Placement agent agreements must require prior written approval for all materials referencing the fund
