# Distribution Policy

**Fund:** The Pinnacle Note Fund
**Effective Date:** [DATE]
**Approved By:** CEO/CIO
**Administered By:** Agent 11 (Cash Controls, Distributions & Treasury)

---

## 1. Distribution Frequency

The fund targets [quarterly] distributions, subject to available distributable cash and the discretion of the fund's manager (CEO/CIO). No distribution is guaranteed. The CEO/CIO may elect to withhold or defer a distribution in any period based on cash availability, reserve requirements, legal obligations, or fund strategy.

Distributions are declared at the CEO/CIO's discretion and may be made more or less frequently than the target cadence if circumstances warrant.

---

## 2. Available Cash — Definition

"Available Cash" for distribution purposes means:

1. **Total Fund Cash** (across all accounts, fund and SPV level)
2. **Less:** Minimum Operating Reserve (defined in Section 3)
3. **Less:** Estimated Fund Expenses for Next [30] Days (servicing fees, legal fees, property expenses, insurance, taxes)
4. **Less:** Any Facility Repayment Due Within [60] Days
5. **Less:** Any Pending Acquisition Commitment (authorized LOI with expected close within [60] days)
6. **Less:** Legal Cost Reserve (defined in Section 3)
7. **Equals:** Available Cash for Distribution

Agent 11 calculates Available Cash before each distribution cycle. CEO/CIO reviews and approves the calculation.

---

## 3. Required Reserves

The following reserves must be maintained at all times. Distributions may not be made that would cause any reserve to fall below its minimum.

| Reserve | Minimum Level | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Operating Cash Reserve | 3 months of projected fund operating expenses | Includes servicing, legal, admin, insurance, misc. |
| Legal Cost Reserve | $[X,000] per active NPL in foreclosure | Adjusted quarterly by Agent 07 estimate |
| Distribution Buffer | 10% above declared distribution amount | Held until wires confirm cleared |
| Facility Repayment Reserve | Any payment due within 60 days | Held in full |

## 4. Preferred Return

[If applicable per fund documents:]

Investors receive a preferred return of 8% per annum (non-compounding) on their contributed capital before the fund manager shares in profits.

Preferred return accrues from the date of each investor's capital contribution and is paid before any other distribution.

---

## 5. Distribution Waterfall

Distributions are paid in the following order per the fund's LPA (European-style waterfall):

1. **Fund expenses and reserves** — operating costs, servicing fees, legal, admin (1.5% annual management fee deducted at fund level)
2. **Return of LP contributed capital** — investors receive return of all contributed capital
3. **Preferred return** — 8% per annum accumulated and unpaid preferred return to investors
4. **Residual** — 80% to LP investors / 20% to fund manager (carried interest)

The exact waterfall mechanics are governed by the LPA. Any ambiguity in the waterfall calculation is resolved by reference to the LPA, with fund counsel engaged if needed.

## 6. Distribution Approval Process

Per the Distribution Process Workflow (WF-06):

1. CEO/CIO declares distribution and authorizes calculation
2. Agent 10 confirms NAV and capital accounts
3. Agent 11 confirms available cash and reserve adequacy
4. Agent 11 produces waterfall calculation
5. Fund administrator independently confirms amounts
6. Agent 14 reviews distribution notice language
7. CEO/CIO approves distribution notice
8. **Dual approval required: CEO/CIO + Controller** before any wire is executed
9. Wires executed by authorized human
10. Distribution logged in approval log and decision log

---

## 7. Investor Reporting on Distributions

Each distribution is accompanied by a distribution notice (prepared by Agent 11, reviewed by Agent 14, approved by CEO/CIO) that includes:

- Distribution amount per investor
- Distribution date
- Source of distribution (income / return of capital / realized gain — per fund documents and tax analysis)
- Year-to-date distribution summary
- Any required tax withholding (coordinate with fund tax advisor)

---

## 8. Restrictions on Distributions

Distributions may not be made under any of the following circumstances without CEO/CIO written authorization with documented rationale:

- Fund cash would fall below minimum operating reserve after distribution
- Any facility covenant would be violated post-distribution
- Any legal obligation (pending judgment, regulatory requirement) requires cash retention
- An active capital call is in process and funds have not been fully deployed
- The fund is in a redemption or wind-down process that restricts distributions

In any restricted period, the CEO/CIO may elect to suspend distributions and must communicate with investors per the fund's governing documents.

[If the fund offers a DRIP or reinvestment option:]

## 10. Record Date

For each distribution, Agent 11 confirms the investor record date — the date on which an investor must be a registered investor to receive the distribution. Record date is set by CEO/CIO at the time of distribution declaration.
