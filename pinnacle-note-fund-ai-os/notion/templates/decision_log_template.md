---

## How to Add an Entry

Copy the format below and paste at the top of the Entries section. Fill in every field — do not skip Reasoning.

```
---
Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
Decision: [What was decided — one clear sentence]
Made By: [Kerry / Kerry + Kody / Full team]
Reasoning: [Why this decision was made — the actual reason, not the polished version]
Context: [What situation or information prompted this — anything future-you would want to know]
Impact: [What this decision affects going forward]
---
```
