## Brand Voice

Standard Black speaks like a serious operator — not a startup, not a bank, not a hustle brand.

**Tone:** Confident. Direct. Credible. Warm when it fits, never soft when it doesn't.

**We sound like:**
- A family office that knows exactly what it's doing
- Someone building something for 25 years, not 25 months
- Intelligent without being condescending
- Plain without being casual

**We do not sound like:**
- A real estate guru or course-seller
- Corporate fluff (synergy, leverage, disrupt)
- Overpromising or hype
- Overly formal or stiff

---

## Writing Rules

**Do:**
- Lead with what you mean
- Use short sentences when making important points
- Write like Kerry speaks — direct, southern, real
- Use numbers and specifics when possible

**Do not:**
- Use hollow jargon: "robust," "innovative," "game-changing," "leverage synergies"
- Add unnecessary disclaimers or softening language
- Over-explain
- Use emojis in professional communications
