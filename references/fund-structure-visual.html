Income SPVs  ·  PN / RPL Pools
  • Acquire cash-flowing performing & re-performing notes
  • Eligible for warehouse financing
  • Primary servicer remits to SPV account
  • Lower risk profile · steady yield
Workout SPVs  ·  NPL Pools
  • Acquire fast-path non-performing notes
  • Separate reserves & advance controls
  • Special servicer + counsel panels
  • No leverage early — optional at scale
Warehouse / Facility SPV  ·  Optional at Scale
  • Holds pledged eligible Income notes as collateral
  • Debt service waterfall takes priority
  • Excess cash flows back upstream to Master
  • Not deployed in early phase — activated when AUM justifies leverage
