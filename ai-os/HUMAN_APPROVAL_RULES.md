# Human Approval Rules

Any action in this category STOPS and waits for Kerry's explicit approval before proceeding. No exceptions.

---

## Requires Kerry's Approval

### Money
- Any transaction, payment, or fund transfer
- Any commitment of team payment pool funds
- Any subscription or service purchase
- Any investment bid, offer, or commitment

### Legal
- Any document with a signature line
- Any contract, agreement, or MOU
- Any entity formation filing
- Any legal counsel engagement

### Investor & LP Communication
- Any email, message, or document sent to an investor or LP
- Any investor deck, PPM, or subscription document
- Any distribution calculation or payment

### External Publishing
- Any content published publicly (LinkedIn, social media, website)
- Any press release or public announcement
- Any ad spend or campaign launch

### Sensitive Data
- Any action involving SSNs, EINs, bank accounts, or personal financial data
- Any action on servicer or borrower records
- Any export or sharing of deal data outside the system

### Deleting Records
- Deleting any file, log, or record that cannot be recovered
- Archiving any active deal or investor record

---

## Approval Process

1. AI flags the action and describes exactly what it is
2. AI states what the outcome will be if approved
3. Kerry gives explicit verbal or written approval
4. AI executes and logs the approval in `10-logs/approval_log.md`

---

## No Implied Approval
A prior approval for one action does not authorize similar future actions. Each instance requires its own approval.
