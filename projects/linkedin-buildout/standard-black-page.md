## ABOUT (2,000 characters max — this version is ready to paste)

Standard Black is an operating company on a long-range mission: building ownership and equity across multiple industries for a new generation of capitalists.

We operate in the fund space — acquiring assets with a disciplined focus on cash flow, downside control, and long-term ownership. As we grow, we expand into the industries that create real economic power: real estate, operating businesses, and capital markets.

Our foundation is built on principles that have always worked — disciplined capital allocation, controlled ownership, and building systems that outlast any individual. We draw from the models of John D. Rockefeller and John Hope Bryant, among others, who understood that capitalism done right creates generational wealth — not just for a few, but for those who are willing to build with intention and discipline.

Standard Black exists because too many people are on the outside of real ownership looking in. We're changing that — one deal, one fund, one business at a time.

We are early. We are intentional. And we are just getting started.

**What we focus on:**
- Fund formation and asset acquisition (primary)
- Operating company ownership
- Equity-based wealth building
- Long-range family-office architecture

If you're an investor, asset seller, broker, or builder — we want to hear from you.
