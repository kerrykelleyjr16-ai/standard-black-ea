Most of what I know, I learned myself — through books, observation, and the people I've been fortunate enough to meet along the way. Every relationship has added something. Every season, even the hard ones, taught me something I couldn't have learned any other way.

That decision became Standard Black.

Our primary focus right now is the fund space: acquiring assets with an emphasis on ownership, cash flow, and generational control. We're early. We're intentional. And we're just getting started.
